Recent projects

The Global Biodiversity Information Facility has a very feature rich portal to access biodiversity primary data. Right now this portal is serving more than 160 million occurrences of more than 1.5 million different taxon. We got the challenge to convert this into an Iphone web application to analyze the feasibility of Iphone UI paradigms to browsing taxonomies. In 3 days we came to the following video and an unofficial Iphone version of the GBIF portal.

With the new Maps for Flash API that Google released there are great new opportunities for displaying interactive data over Google Maps due to the Flash platform. You can see our dynamically created HeatMaps from GBIF data. Other example where we had the possibility to tried was combining the World Database on Protected Areas with information from Wikipedia, Flickr and Panoramio. Check out the protected area explorer for Tanzania.
