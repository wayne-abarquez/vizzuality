Data visualization services

Yes, we like beautiful data visualization, but we do not forget its main purposes: communicate and inspire

Biodiversity informatics is producing huge amounts of data every day. Unfortunately most of this data is useless withouth effective ways to analyze it. To convey ideas eficiely, both aesthetic form and functionality need to go hand in hand, providing insights into a rather sparse and complex data set by communicatiing its key-aspects in a more intuitive way.

Our expertise in data visualization for biodiversity includes: observation data, taxonomic trees, interactive keys, temporal datasets and many more. Contact us to check if we can help you with your speciﬁc use case!
