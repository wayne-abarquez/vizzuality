Job Overview

IUCN and Google have partnered in an initiative to make information about Marine Protected Areas (MPAs) available to a global audience in ‘Oceans in Google Earth’, available through Google Earth v5.0. This layer contains both exciting multimedia content and core data on MPAs that is critical to our global monitoring efforts.
