  • Description

  • The seagrass dataset has been compiled by UNEP-WCMC in collaboration with Dr Frederick T. Short, University of New Hampshire, USA to show the global distribution of seagrass species. This dataset shows the range of individual seagrass species and was published in "The World Atlas of Seagrasses" (2003). This dataset was used to create a global map of seagrass biodiversity.
