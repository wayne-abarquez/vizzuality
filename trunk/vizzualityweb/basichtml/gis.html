Rich Geographic Information Systems

What, When and Where is key in biodiversity. Visualizing these three components in Rich Geographic Information applications is what we do best.

Since Google Maps appeared, the world of online maps just got much more interesting. We love mashups on top of these new maps APIs. Together with the use of spatial standards like the ones from OGC, we can build engaging maps experiences that invite users to explore your data.

We have worked displaying millions of primary data occurrences, creating heat maps, dynamic grouping data. We call it BIGBI, Biodiversity Informatics Geospatial Business InTelligence. Let us work with your POINTS and POLYGONS and we will impress you can do with them!

Rich GIS technologies

We love Google Maps API and we have been using since it appeared. With the new Google Maps API for ﬂash we can do incredible things.

Our favorite open source geospatial server. We use Geoserver stack of technologies to support OGC standards and connect to Rich Mapping APIs.

Together with the best open source relational database, PostgreSQL, we use PostGIS to analyze and serve geospatial data.
