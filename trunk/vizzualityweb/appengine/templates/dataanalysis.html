Recent case study

The Global Biodiversity Information Facility collect and index millions of primary data records. The project required the cross referecing of million of points against the database of World Protected Areas maintained by the UNEP World Conservation Monitoring Centre. This is 160.000 polygons against 160 million points. By using Amazon Computing in the cloud web services, S3 and EC2, we constraucted a reliable process to update the analytical data needed to display in widgets. This data is now accesible through the World Database on Protected Areas
