Rich Geographic Information Systems

What, When and Where are key in biodiversity. Visualizing these three components in Rich Geographic Information applications is what we do best.

The emergence of Google Maps has allowed for a new level of mapping applications on the web. We love mashups on top of these new maps APIs. Together with the use of spatial standards such as the Open Geospatial Standards, we can build engaging maps experiences that invite users to explore your data while interoperating with external sources through WMS, WFS, etc.

We work on displaying millions of primary data occurrences, creating heat maps and dynamically grouping data. Through innovative use of pyramid gridding techniques we have successfully enabled to play interactive maps for variable resolution for hundreds of millions of point data.
